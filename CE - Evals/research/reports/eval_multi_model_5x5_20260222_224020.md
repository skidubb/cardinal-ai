# Protocol Evaluation: Multi-Model 5×5


## Evaluation Design

### Questions

- **Q1.1:** PLG tier alongside consulting
- **Q2.1:** Competitor raised $20M, offering free audits
- **Q3.2:** Client concentration — 40% revenue client demands 25% discount
- **Q4.3:** Client satisfaction scores are 4.7/5.0 but renewal rate dropped from 80% to 55% this year. Exit interviews cite 'budget constraints' but our pricing hasn't increased. What's actually going on?
- **Q7.1:** How do we scale without hiring when our value proposition is human expertise applied through AI? Every scaling mechanism (PLG, automation, templates) dilutes the 'bespoke' positioning that commands premium pricing.

### Protocols

- **Parallel Synthesis (Control)** — Multiple agents respond independently in a single pass, then outputs are synthesized. Closest to 'single agent + context' baseline.
- **Multi-Round Debate** — Agents engage in 3 rounds of iterative debate where positions evolve and sharpen through disagreement.
- **ACH (Analysis of Competing Hypotheses)** — Structured intelligence methodology: agents generate hypotheses, evaluate evidence for/against each, then converge.
- **Red Team / Blue Team / White Team** — Adversarial protocol: Red attacks the strategy, Blue defends, White adjudicates unresolved tensions.
- **Borda Count** — Agents rank strategic options independently, aggregated via Borda voting to surface collective preference.

### Judging Criteria (1-5 scale)

- **Answer Quality:** Does the response directly and thoroughly answer the question asked?
- **Reasoning Depth:** Are claims supported by evidence, logic chains, or structured analysis rather than assertions?
- **Tension Surfacing:** Does the output identify genuine trade-offs and conflicting considerations, not just list perspectives?
- **Actionability:** Are there concrete next steps with owners, timelines, or decision criteria?
- **Novelty:** Does the response surface non-obvious insights or reframe the problem in a useful way?
- **Coherence:** Is the output internally consistent, well-structured, and free of contradictions?

## The Big Picture

Cardinal Element sits at the intersection of two accelerating forces: an $18B+ AI consulting market growing at 24% CAGR, and enterprise demand for AI implementation that has outpaced the supply of qualified partners. You have the right positioning, the right capabilities, and the right market timing. This plan is designed for a founder-led firm: high-leverage moves, minimal overhead, maximum revenue per hour invested.
